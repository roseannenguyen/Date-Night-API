How to Use Date Night
  • Date Night is a "dinner and a movie" suggestion tool. You pick a movie and we'll suggest a place for dinner. And not just any place, we scour through top-rated restaurants in the area to offer recommendations that are sure to impress. After all, this is for date night!
  • Helpful Links can assist you in getting the where-when-and-how for the movie of your choice, especially if you're looking for oldies or a classic.
  • Now before you start your search, we also want to mention that Date Night is pretty handy when you're traveling. We locate eateries by zip code, so the Date Night app can help you locate good food across town or across the country.
